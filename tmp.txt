To play **"Embers and Echoes"** on the guitar, you can use these classic chords that work perfectly for a campfire setting.

For the "enchanting" verses, use a light, steady strumming pattern. For the "rock n roll" chorus, use more power and a driving rhythm.

### **Chords Needed:**

* **G Major** (320033)
* **D Major** (xx0232)
* **Em (E Minor)** (022000)
* **C Major** (x32010)

---

### **Chord Progression:**

---

### **Quick Tips for the Scout Event:**

* **The Verse:** Keep it intimate. Use down-strums to let the lyrics about the "starry sky" breathe.
* **The Chorus:** This is where the energy picks up! Increase your strumming speed and volume. If you want a more "rock" sound, you can swap the open chords for **Power Chords** (G5 - D5 - E5 - C5) during the chorus.
* **The Transition:** Right before the chorus starts (after "always tell"), do a quick build-up on the **C Major** chord to lead everyone into the big sing-along.

Conseils de jeu :
Le tempo : Gardez un rythme soutenu (environ 128 BPM).

L'accentuation : Sur le refrain, n'hésitez pas à bien marquer le premier temps de chaque accord pour donner ce côté "hymne" qui rassemble tout le groupe.

Le final : Terminez sur un grand accord de Sol Majeur (G) bien vibrant pour clore la chanson en beauté !
